About the Course

Turn your DJ skills into a career.

Level 3 at Soundabode is where artists evolve - from mixing in the studio to performing on real stages.

This advanced mentorship-based program helps you craft your signature style, build your artist profile, and establish a professional presence in India's fast-growing music scene.

You'll perform at live events, record mixtapes, and get trained in artist branding, stage management, and entrepreneurship for DJs - with direct guidance from top-performing artists and industry mentors.

With unlimited practice hours, event exposure, and mentorship from working professionals, you'll graduate with more than just skill - you'll have an artist identity.

Frequently Asked Questions

Q1. What is the Professional DJ / Performance Path? +
It's a mentorship and performance-driven program that transforms DJs into full-fledged performing artists with a clear career roadmap, including artist branding, live performance opportunities, and industry networking.
Q2. Do I need to complete Level 2 before joining? +
Yes, or you should have equivalent experience in mixing and performance basics. Level 3 is designed for intermediate to advanced DJs ready to go professional.
Q3. Will I actually perform live? +
Yes! You'll perform at Soundabode's partner venues and student showcase nights. Live performance experience is a core part of the Level 3 curriculum.
Q4. Does Soundabode help with artist branding and promotion? +
Absolutely. You'll create an electronic press kit (EPK), record professional mixtapes, get artist photos, and learn social media strategies for building your brand and fanbase.
Q5. Can I get paid gigs after this course? +
Yes. Many of our trainees secure paid gigs, residencies, and event collaborations through our partner network. Level 3 prepares you for professional bookings and teaches you fee negotiation and client relations.
