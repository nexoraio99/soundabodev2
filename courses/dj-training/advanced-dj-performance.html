About the Program

From DJ to professional performing artist.

Level 3 is Soundabode’s most advanced DJ pathway, designed for DJs who want real performance exposure, branding support, and long-term artist growth.

This is not just a course — it is a mentorship program focused on stage performance, artist identity, and industry readiness.

Program Objectives

  • Develop a strong professional DJ identity.
  • Perform live at clubs and partner venues.
  • Create mixtapes and promotional content.
  • Learn artist branding and audience building.
  • Understand DJ entrepreneurship and contracts.

What You Will Master

  • Live performance strategy and crowd control.
  • Stage presence and performance confidence.
  • Branding, press kits and artist promotion.
  • Networking with clubs and event organizers.
  • Long-term career planning as a DJ.

Career Outcomes

  • Club & Festival DJ.
  • Resident DJ.
  • Touring Artist.
  • Independent DJ Brand.

Program Details

  • Duration: 3–4 months.
  • Mode: Mentorship + Live gigs.
  • Location: Soundabode Academy, Pune.
