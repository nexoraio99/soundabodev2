About the Course

Turn your passion for music into a performance skill.

At Soundabode, Level 1 Basic DJ Training is the perfect first step for anyone who dreams of mixing, performing, and commanding the dance floor.

Over 2 months of intensive, hands-on sessions, you will learn how to mix tracks seamlessly, understand beat structures, master EQ control, and build your first live DJ set.

The course combines technical mastery with creative expression, so you do not just learn to mix, you learn to perform with style and confidence.

Course Objectives

  • Build strong foundations in beatmatching, EQing, and track transitioning.
  • Learn the Pioneer CDJ and DJM mixer workflow.
  • Understand music structure, timing, and harmonics.
  • Gain confidence performing a complete DJ set.
  • Develop a personal sense of genre, taste, and mixing style.

Skills You Will Master

  • Beatmatching and tempo control.
  • EQ balancing and smooth transition techniques.
  • Track management in Rekordbox and Traktor.
  • Basic FX and filter usage.
  • Building and recording your first DJ set.

What You Will Learn

Condensed Overview

  • Beatmatching and cueing: counting beats, syncing tempos, and dropping tracks on time.
  • Track selection and EQing: understanding frequencies, intros and outros, and clean transitions.
  • Harmonic mixing: using Camelot chart and key compatibility for musical blends.
  • Digital setup basics: Rekordbox, Traktor, and MIDI controllers.
  • Performance confidence: building your first mix and recording it live.

Frequently Asked Questions

Q1. Do I need any music experience before joining? +
No experience is needed. Level 1 starts from scratch and covers everything from beat counting and tempo awareness to mixing your first set.

You will train on Pioneer CDJs and professional DJM mixers, along with Rekordbox and Traktor software and compatible controllers.
