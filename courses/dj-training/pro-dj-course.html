About the Course

Step into the world of professional DJing.

Level 2 at Soundabode is where you move beyond the basics of beatmatching and explore the nuances of performance. Over four months, you'll dive deep into advanced techniques that define club-ready sets.

From harmonic mixing and FX layering to mastering digital workflows like Traktor and Rekordbox, this program is designed to make you versatile and confident behind any console.

You'll develop your unique sound identity while learning how to troubleshoot live setups and read any crowd energy. This is the level where you transform into a performer, gaining real confidence through guided practice and feedback from working DJs.

With access to Pioneer CDJs, DJM mixers, and Ableton Live, you'll be training on the exact equipment used in the world's best clubs and festivals.

Course Objectives

  • Master advanced mixing & transition styles.
  • In-depth FX layering & filter techniques.
  • Professional harmonic (key) mixing mastery.
  • Digital DJ setups with 4-deck control.
  • Crowd reading & set energy design skills.
  • Artist branding foundations & set recordings.
  • Real-world gig preparation & technical setup.

What You Will Learn

Condensed Overview

  • Advanced Mixing: Layering echo outs, filter sweeps, and creative build-ups.
  • Harmonic Mixing: Mastering the Camelot wheel and key detection workflows.
  • Digital Ecosystem: Deep dive into Rekordbox, Traktor, and controller mapping.
  • Performance Strategy: Building energy curves and managing long set formats.
  • Technical Mastery: Mastering EQ, gain staging, and booth monitoring.
  • Branding: Creating your artist profile and recording professional mixtapes.
