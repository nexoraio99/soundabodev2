What You Will Learn

A condensed view of the core topics covered in the Pro DJ Training Course.

Advanced Mixing And FX

  • Layering transitions, echo outs and filter sweeps.
  • Creative build ups and breakdowns for different genres.
  • Using FX to shape tension and release on the dance floor.

Harmonics And Key Mixing

  • Camelot chart mastery and key detection.
  • Avoiding clashes and clashing chords in transitions.
  • Key shifting and musical phrasing for emotional impact.

Digital DJing And Controllers

  • Traktor, Rekordbox and Ableton integration workflows.
  • Controller and HID techniques with Novation, Allen & Heath, Traktor.
  • Music management, playlist curation and export for gigs.

Live Performance Preparation

  • Reading crowd energy and adjusting on the fly.
  • Building BPM and energy curves for different set lengths.
  • EQ mastery for club systems and monitoring environments.

Frequently Asked Questions

Q1. Who can join the Pro DJ course?+
Anyone who has completed Level 1 or already has basic mixing experience can join directly. If you are unsure, we can assess your current level in a short demo session.
Q2. Is this course suitable for club DJs?+
Yes. Level 2 is designed specifically to prepare you for club, festival and event performance setups, including realistic practice on club style sound systems.
Q3. Do I get to perform during the course?+
Yes. Every trainee performs at least once during our student showcase events and partner venue nights, with feedback from mentors on set design and crowd control.
Q4. Do you teach Traktor and Rekordbox both?+
Yes. You will learn both Traktor and Rekordbox, plus HID mode and Ableton Live integration so that you can perform confidently on different rigs.
Q5. Will Soundabode help me get gigs?+
Absolutely. Through our industry network and venue partnerships we create opportunities for students to perform professionally and start building a track record.
