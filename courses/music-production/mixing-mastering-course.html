Frequently Asked Questions

Difference between mixing and mastering?+
Mixing balances individual tracks; mastering finalizes the overall sound and loudness for distribution.
Do I need my own software?+
No. All training uses our studio setup with Ableton Live, Ozone, and Waves plug-ins.
Can I bring my own music?+
Yes, we encourage you to work on your own projects under expert guidance.
