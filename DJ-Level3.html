About the Course

Turn your DJ skills into a career.

Level 3 at Soundabode is where artists evolve from mixing in the studio to performing on real stages. This advanced mentorship based program helps you craft your signature style, build your artist profile, and establish a professional presence in India's fast growing music scene.

You will perform at live events, record mixtapes, and get trained in artist branding, stage management, and DJ entrepreneurship with guidance from top performing artists and industry mentors.

The program blends career strategy, performance mastery, and industry networking to prepare you for real world success as a touring or resident DJ.

Course Objectives

  • Build a complete artist profile and electronic press kit (EPK).
  • Record multiple mixtapes across different genres and moods.
  • Learn venue management, event coordination, and stage ethics.
  • Prepare for residencies, club performances, and live events.
  • Master DJ entrepreneurship, fee negotiation, and client relations.
  • Build a strong social media and brand presence as an artist.

Skills You Will Master

  • Live performance confidence and crowd control.
  • Artist branding, portfolio design, and EPK creation.
  • Social media growth and booking strategy.
  • Technical troubleshooting in live setups.
  • Entrepreneurship, pricing, and professional etiquette.

What You Will Learn

Condensed Overview

  • Professional performance prep: building full live sets and managing crowd energy.
  • Artist branding: crafting your image, press releases, and promo kits.
  • Networking: making industry connections and finding bookings.
  • Residency skills: managing venues, performing consistently, and building a fan base.
  • Troubleshooting: solving setup, controller, and software issues on the fly.
  • Career management: setting fees, handling PR, and maintaining artist ethics.
