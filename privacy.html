Privacy Policy

Last Updated: October 2025

At Soundabode, we value your trust and are committed to protecting your personal data. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you visit our website or enroll in our programs.

1. Information We Collect

We collect information to deliver an exceptional learning experience, improve our offerings, and maintain platform integrity.

  • Personal Data: Full name, email address, phone number, and city or country provided during enrollment.
  • Payment Details: Transaction records when registering for a course or event.
  • Automatic Data: Browser type, device info, IP address, and usage patterns collected via cookies and pixels.
  • Creative Assets: Projects or audio files shared for mentoring or portfolio reviews.

2. How We Use Your Data

We use your information only for legitimate educational and business purposes:

  • Processing course enrollments and issuing certificates.
  • Communicating schedules, workshops, and industry updates.
  • Providing technical and creative mentoring.
  • Analyzing engagement to enhance the learning curriculum.
  • Ensuring platform security and legal compliance.

3. Data Sharing

We never sell or rent your personal data. We only share information with trusted partners for essential services:

  • Service Partners: Hosting, analytics, and payment processors (Razorpay, Google, Meta).
  • Collaborative Partners: Brands like Ableton or Apple for verified student programs.
  • Legal Authorities: When required by law to protect Soundabode's rights and integrity.

4. Data Retention & Rights

We retain data only as long as necessary for educational purposes. You have the right to access, correct, or request the deletion of your personal data at any time.

For students in the EU/UK, we process data under GDPR guidelines including contractual necessity and legitimate interest.

5. Children's Privacy

Our programs are intended for individuals aged 13 and above. Students under 18 must be registered by a parent or legal guardian.

Privacy Questions?

For any data requests or privacy concerns, please contact our team at services@soundabode.com or WhatsApp us at +91 9975016189.
